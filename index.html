How to register

Step 1

Request an invite from Kanteron Systems. Once you receive the invitation...

You should have received an email with an invitation. Once you receive the invitation open it and click on "Get Started"

Step 2

Accept the invitation

You have to click on green "Accept". Then you will be redirected to next step

Step 3

Create your account

You have to click on "Create My Account". Then you will be redirected to next step

Step 4

Fill your data

Fill your personal data. Remember your username and your password because you will need them to enter our platform.
